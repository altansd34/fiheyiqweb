What the Actors From Iconic Romantic Movies of the 80s and 90s Look Like Today

At the end of the previous century, romantic movies were really popular, and even people that didn’t like romantic flicks watched them because of the actors. Either way, no matter how many years pass, these actors always remain as handsome as they were in the ’80s and the ’90s.

Bright Side decided to find out what the actors from the leading roles in old romantic movies look like today.

Andrew McCarthy, 58 years old

Billy Crystal, 73 years old

  • When Harry Met Sally (1989)

Hugh Grant, 61 years old

  • Four Weddings and a Funeral (1994), Sense and Sensibility (1995), Notting Hill (1999)

Ethan Hawke, 50 years old

Nicolas Cage, 57 years old
