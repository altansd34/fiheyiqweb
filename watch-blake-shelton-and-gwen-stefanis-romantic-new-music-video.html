Voice coaches Blake Shelton and Gwen Stefani just keep giving us amazing duets to listen to. The music video for their latest song, “Nobody But You,” was just released, and we love it.

The couple teased the video on Instagram earlier this week, and they had us itching to watch it. Watch the full music video below, and learn more about the song.

Blake Shelton And Gwen Stefani Tease Music Video

Both Blake and Gwen posted clips from the music video on their Instagram pages. Then today, they finally released it! The video features the couple sharing French fries, cuddling on a sofa with their dog, and singing in fancy outfits.

BLAKE SHELTON AND GWEN STEFANI WANT TO GET MARRIED BUT THIS IS STOPPING THEM

The clip also includes some moody shots of a forest, with Blake walking through with the same dog, and Gwen dancing in a flowing gown. They end the video driving together in a car, with a few cute Instagram photos edited in.

What Is Blake And Gwen’s Song About?

This is Blake and Gwen’s third duet together, after “Go Ahead and Break My Heart” and “You Make It Feel Like Christmas.” Blake has described it as “our song,” and the lyrics are tender and romantic.

GWEN STEFANI TALKS BLAKE SHELTON HEALING HER AFTER DIVORCE

“I don’t wanna live without you / I don’t wanna even breathe,” they sing in the chorus. “I don’t wanna dream about you / Wanna wake up with you next to me / I don’t wanna go down any other road now / I don’t wanna love nobody but you / Looking in your eyes now, if I had to die now / I don’t wanna love nobody but you.”

We’re excited to finally see the couple’s music video for the track, but that’s not the only thing to be happy about. According to iHeartRadio, Blake and Gwen are going to perform “Nobody But You” at the Grammys later this month.

Tune in Sunday, January 26, 2020, at 8 p.m. ET on CBS to watch them take the stage.
