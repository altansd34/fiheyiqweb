Hungarian Blogger Recreates Iconic Looks Inspired By Movies and TV Shows

Kate (@kkpiece) is a Hungarian fashion blogger who took on a very creative challenge: create stylish outfits inspired by different characters from popular movies, television shows, and even anime. The best part is that these looks are simple enough that you could try and recreate them at home, yet so adorable that they could be used for many different occasions.

Bright Side was amazed by every single outfit and that’s why we decided to share them with you.
