'DWTS' Pro Sharna Burgess Writes Sweet Letter to Son Zane

TALENTRECAP.COM Talent Dancing With The Stars

Sharna Burgess via Instagram

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Dancing With The Stars pro Sharna Burgess recently wrote a touching open letter for her son, Zane. She and her partner Brian Austin Green welcomed their baby boy in 2022.

Sharna Burgess Posts Adorable Message for Her Baby Boy

In a recently uploaded video on Instagram, Burgess looked back at a moment where she was still pregnant with Zane and showed how much he has grown since then. According to the DWTS pro, she’s proven herself wrong when she thought she couldn’t love her son more and more.

“My Mr Man, my beautiful babbling giggly and very opinionated boy,” she wrote. “You wanna know something I am so certain of now that I have brought a life into this world? It’s that they turn up who they are already. Their isms, quirks, personality … it’s a part of the soul that lives within the body.”

While Burgess recognizes that no parent is perfect, she believes that they must guide their children as they form their own beliefs. The dancer also noted that her role should be to create a “safe loving environment” that’s meant to nurture Zane.

“All we can do is our best, to not control or create or change them, but to understand they are the writer director and star of their own movie and we are simply their supporting role,” she wrote.

She ended her post by claiming that being a mom is “by far the greatest most rewarding job” ever. At present, Burgess assumes the role of a mother to Green’s three sons with Megan Fox.

She Recently Shared a Motherhood Update

Before Christmas, the DWTS pro shared an update about her breastfeeding journey. She posted a series of photos that she’s taken when she’s feeding milk to Zane.

“My supply seems to be slowing so much even though I’m doing all the things,” she wrote. “I’ll be so sad when I can’t nurse him anymore and at the same time happy to have my boobs be mine again.”

Moreover, Burgess stated that the six-month-old Zane is now babbling and eating food. As mentioned by the dancer, her baby boy resonates well with his zodiac sign, Cancer. Particularly, Zane is emotional, sensitive and needs love at all times.
