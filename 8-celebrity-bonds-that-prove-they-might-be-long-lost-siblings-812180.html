8 Celebrity Bonds That Prove They Might Be Long Lost Siblings

Actors and actresses are used to playing any relationship on screen, including romances and friendships. However, some of our favorite celebrities have managed to transfer their friendship from the screen to real life. We adore all the support, admiration, and love these people found in each other.

1. Selena Gomez and Taylor Swift

Gilbert Flores/Broadimage/EAST NEWS, Gilbert Flores /Broadimage/EAST NEWS

Selena Gomez and Taylor Swift have the type of friendship that we all wish for. They have known each other for over a decade, and their relationship is filled with support and admiration. “It’s been the longest [friendship] I think either of us had, really,” Taylor once shared. “Longevity is something you really can find very precious and rare in friendships.”

2. Kristen Stewart and Dakota Fanning

Sometimes, friendship is not just about hanging out together. It’s about growing and having each other’s back. And that is the case for Kristen Stewart and Dakota Fanning. Once, Dakota had the honor of introducing Kristen at an award show. And during her speech, Fanning shared her gratitude and admiration toward her friend.

“She has held my hair back and talked me through heartbreak,” Dakota said. “She has always been there for me when I have needed her most, and most of all, she has taught me the power of being one hundred percent yourself one hundred percent of the time.”

3. Maisie Williams and Sophie Turner

White Label/East News, DDNY/Broadimage/Broad Image/East News

And the same can be said for Maisie Williams and Sophie Turner. Coming from quite similar backgrounds and going through a similar path of growing up simultaneously on- and off-screen, with thousands of people watching, they had each other’s back. “It’s nice to have her going through this industry, she’s someone who just completely gets it,” said Maisie. “It’s really lovely to have someone hold my hand through all of it.”

4. Emma Stone and Jennifer Lawrence

AFP/EAST NEWS

For Emma Stone and Jennifer Lawrence, their friendship is not limited by the walls of Hollywood studios or the length of the red carpet. They are there for each other off-screen and would probably still be friends even if they weren’t famous actresses. “We both really do love each other and care about each other as people beyond being actors,” said Jennifer. I support her completely when it comes to work and I feel the same from her, but I know we’d be friends even if we didn’t do the same job."

5. Tobey Maguire and Leonardo DiCaprio

Bellak Rachid/ABACA/EAST NEWS

Leonardo DiCaprio and Tobey Maguire have been friends for more than 30 years already, meeting for the first time in 1990. Of course, throughout such a long friendship, they supported each other in both their personal life and their career, building “trust and respect for each other.” However, what also keeps their friendship vivid is their casual relationship.

“We are like any other buddies. There’s a lot of laughing and chatting between us,” shared Tobey. “We enjoy doing a lot of things we have in common, like basketball. We like to play and talk about basketball.”

6. Jennifer Aniston and Paul Rudd

AP/East News

Jennifer Aniston and Paul Rudd worked together on several projects, including even Friends, as Rudd joined the show as a side character in later seasons. And them working together is “a lot of laughter,” as Jennifer recalled.

“I love him. He is a dear friend,” she said. “We just have a true affection for each other, and I just feel honored any time I get to work with him.”

7. Ryan Reynolds and Hugh Jackman

Invision/Invision/East News

Ryan Reynolds and Hugh Jackman are best friends, or maybe it’s better to say, best frienemies. They have been close since 2009, when they worked together on the movie X-Men Origins: Wolverine. Since then, their friendship has become the most hilarious feud that thousands of people enjoy, and they don’t intend to stop.

8. Jamie Lee Curtis and Michelle Yeoh

Jamie Lee Curtis has found this special bond of a true friendship with Michelle Yeoh, and they are totally “in love” with one another. At a recent SAG Awards, where Curtis won the Best Supporting Female Actor award, Curtis kissed her co-star, Yeoh, to celebrate the moment.

“I kissed her? Did I really kiss her?” Jamie joked later. “I love Michelle Yeoh. We love each other. We fell in love with each other. She’s married. I met her husband in England, he’s lovely. I also have a husband.”

Kate Winslet has provided insight into the dynamics of her intimate scenes with Leonardo DiCaprio, highlighting the blend of passion and friendship. Despite their close bond, filming intimate moments for “Revolutionary Road” was challenging for Winslet, especially with the film being directed by her then-husband, Sam Mendes. She described the experience as “weird,” given her husband’s presence during such scenes.
