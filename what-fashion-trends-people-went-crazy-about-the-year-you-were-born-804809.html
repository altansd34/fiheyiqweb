What Fashion Trends People Went Crazy About the Year You Were Born

Fashion changes really fast, and what looked trendy only a decade ago may look somewhat ridiculous nowadays. From colorful geometry patterns of the ’60s and ’70s to voluminous shoulders of the ’80s and skinny eyebrows of the ’90s, we’ve seen so many fashion trends, and it’s time to recall them all.

We at Bright Side looked through photo archives of several past decades to find out which trends people followed year by year, and here’s the list we came up with. Check it out to see what people looked like the year you were born.

2015: motorcycle jackets of all colors

2014: dresses with bold cutouts

2013: Office suits were worn everywhere, including red carpet events.

2012: animal prints in all variations

2011: very large bags

2010: leather dresses

2009: peplum tops, skirts, and dresses

2008: hands-free bags with chain straps

2007: vests worn for almost any occasion

2006: very short shorts

2005: halter dresses and tops

2004: 1920s-inspired bohemian dresses

2003: corsets with lace in front

2002: low-rise jeans

2001: sequins

2000: slip dresses

1999: choker necklaces

1998: blue eyeshadow

1997: short haircuts on women

1996: scrunchies, clips, and other visible hair accessories

1995: large golden earrings

1994: skinny eyebrows

1993: bold color combinations

1992: romantic dresses with combat or cowboy boots

1991: off-the-shoulder dresses

1990: shiny fabrics

1989: cropped blazers

1988: voluminous tresses

1987: voluminous sleeves with shoulder pads

1986: wrap dresses with voluminous shoulder pads

1985: gravity defying hairstyles

1984: aerobics-inspired outfits

1983: polka dots

1982: outfits with ruffled collars

1981: floral prints

1980: sparkling outfits

1975-1979: many different prints combined in one outfit and double-breasted dresses with buttons

1970-1974: crocheted cardigans and vests and abstract prints

1965-1969: geometry in all shapes and colors

1960-1964: little black dress

So what fashion trends did people follow the year you were born? Do you like these trends?
