Who is Rumer Willis?

Rumer Willis was born on August 16, 1988, in Paducah, Kentucky to Bruce Willis and Demi Moore. Her name Rumer came from the British novelist Rumer Godden.

They eventually moved to Hailey, Idaho, where she grew up.

Rumer has two younger biological sisters, Scout LaRue and Tallulah Belle, born in 1991 and 1994, respectively.

Bruce Willis daughters from his marriage to Emma Heming are Mable Ray and Evelyn Penn. They are Rumer's paternal half-sisters.

Life was never easy growing under the limelight. Bruce Willis kids felt the pressure of their parents being Hollywood icons as well as the inconsistency of their parent's relationship.

Education

She attended Michigan Interlochen Arts Academy for her elementary studies. She then entered Wildwood Secondary School in Los Angeles.

After graduating from high school, she enrolled at the University of Southern California. However, it did not last as she dropped out after one semester to concentrate on her booming acting career.
