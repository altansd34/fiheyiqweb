Beach House Hywel Griffith | AA Restaurant of the Year

Beach House has one of the best seafront locations in Wales: it's an old coal shed, right on the sands at Oxwich on the south coast of Gower, converted into an airy modern restaurant. It also has some of the best food, too, thanks to the hyper-local, hyper-seasonal approach of head chef Hywel Griffith.
