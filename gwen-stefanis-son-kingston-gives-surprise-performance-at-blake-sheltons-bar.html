Gwen Stefani's Son Kingston Gives Surprise Performance at Blake Shelton's Bar

TALENTRECAP.COM Entertainment Celebrity

Getty Images

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Gwen Stefani’s eldest son with ex-husband Gavin Rossdale, Kingston, has recently made his live music debut at Blake Shelton’s bar in Oklahoma. The 17-year-old rising star gave a surprise solo performance last Friday, and his stepdad couldn’t be prouder.

Kingston Rossdale Gives Surprise Performance at Stepdad’s Oklahoma Bar

In a video shared on TikTok, Kingston Rossdale can be seen standing onstage at Shelton’s Ole Red in Tishomingo, Oklahoma. Seconds before he kicked off his song number, Kingston confidently talked to the audience and a band member alongside him. He then belted out the song, commanding the crowd with his promising vocals.

“Love you guys, thank you for being here!” he said. “Thank you, it means everything to me, really. I hope you guys had a great night, enjoy yourselves!”

Clearly, Shelton was a proud step-dad, giving Kingston a big hug after he finished his set. Ahead of the 17-year-old’s performance, the country crooner teased Kingston’s live debut on X (formerly Twitter) and invited people to show up on Friday. 

There’s nothing shocking in knowing that Kingston has a knack for singing. Being a genetic product of a pop icon and rockstar, Kingston’s musical talent runs rapidly in his blood. Not to mention that he’s got a supportive stepfather who’s a country music veteran.

Stefani, Shelton Also Performed During the Night

Kingston was not the only member of the Shelton family who graced the stage that night. His lovely mom and stepdad also took the time to show off their musical prowess at Ole Red. Having a sweet moment before their number, the couple performed No Doubt’s hit song “Don’t Speak.”

“POV: you live in the same small town as Blake Shelton & Gwen Stefani and get to see them perform together EVERY YEAR,” the user who uploaded their video on TikTok wrote.

In July, rumors claiming that Stefani and Shelton’s “relationship is suffering” surfaced. There have also been claims stating that the “Hollaback Girl” hitmaker is seeking couples therapy to save their marriage. Such unverified reports, however, seem contrary to the intimacy that the couple’s romance is “drifting apart.“
