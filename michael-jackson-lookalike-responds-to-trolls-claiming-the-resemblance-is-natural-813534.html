Michael Jackson Lookalike Responds to Trolls Claiming the Resemblance Is Natural

Mirror, mirror on the wall, who’s the true Michael Jackson of them all? Meet Fabio Jackson, the 28-year-old King of Pop lookalike. Not only does he bear an uncanny resemblance, but he also has the moves to match. Despite facing skeptics and online trolls claiming he went under the knife, Fabio remains unfazed. To silence the naysayers, he fearlessly shared his childhood pictures, proving that his striking likeness is genuine and dispelling any plastic surgery rumors.

Fabio effortlessly embodies the King of Pop.

This UK-based sensation, now a professional MJ lookalike and influencer, Fabio causes quite a commotion. People do double-takes and even snap pictures, thinking they’ve just spotted MJ in the flesh. Not only that his facial features and hairstyle are just like MJ, but he is also an incredibly talented dancer who effortlessly channels the music icon’s trademark dance moves.

Childhood snapshots as proof of natural likeness.

Of course, fame comes with its fair share of online negativity — from fighting skeptics and standing up to internet trolls who accused him of undergoing plastic surgeries to look like MJ. On the other hand, Fabio claims that the MJ magic has been in his DNA since childhood, and he’s never had any work done to achieve that look.

Fabio claims his face is completely natural and has proof to back it up. As he puts it, his jaw has grown wider, his teeth have shifted, and his eyes are still the same. He recently stunned the TikTok audience with a video featuring his childhood snapshots.

Fans couldn’t help but claim that Fabio is the original Michael Jackson. Some even playfully suggest that MJ might’ve taken a few style cues from him.

He’s not even a hard-core Michael Jackson fan.

Fabio may not call himself a die-hard MJ fan, but he deeply admires the late pop star’s dedication to charitable causes. Michael has been more than just an inspiration to him; he’s been a teacher: ’’I’m shy in the real world, but I love performing and dancing like Michael. When I’m being creative, nothing else matters,’’ the TikTok star admits.

Despite all the negativity, Fabio is not backing down.

Fame has brought Fabio thousands of fans but also taught him how to deal with mean comments: ’’I’m not going to do anything when people are mean to me online. There are so many people out there that wish to be Michael or just had a small percentage of this resemblance to him,’’ he says, adding that his aim is to spread a ’’positive influence.’’

From ordinary individuals to famous personalities, the doppelgänger phenomenon never fails to amaze us, leaving us questioning the existence of hidden connections. Whether it’s A-list actors or renowned musicians, the similarities can be close to unbelievable, just like in Fabio’s case.
