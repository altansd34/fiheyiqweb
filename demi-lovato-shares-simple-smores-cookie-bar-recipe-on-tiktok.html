Demi Lovato Shares Simple Smores Cookie Bar Recipe on TikTok

TALENTRECAP.COM Entertainment Celebrity

2021 [iHeartMedia]

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Pop superstar Demi Lovato revealed that they’ve been addicted to cooking and baking in the past few days. The “Heart Attack” hitmaker recently brought their fans along in the kitchen, to share how they make appetizing s’mores cookie bars.

Demi Lovato Says They’re Into Cooking, Baking Lately

In a TikTok vlog titled “Make s’mores cookie bars with me,” Lovato updated their fans about what they have been up to lately. The kitchen is slowly becoming Lovato’s happy place.

“I’ve been finding so much joy in cooking and baking lately,” they wrote. 

Wearing their comfy Megadeth shirt, the pop singer began by showing the ingredients for the baking process. They then proceeded to mix the batter with the rest needed for making s’mores cookie bars. Subsequently, the singer finished preparing the chewy squares by putting Hershey’s chocolate on top of it.

“Send me recipe ideas in the comments!!” they wrote.

With Lovato’s caption, their followers were generous enough to give them ideas. Suggestions consist of cheesecake, rice krispy treats, and crumble cookie bars.

Fans are Glad to See Them in a Happy State

Witnessing Lovato in their “TikTok era,” fans are clearly delighted that they have found a new hobby. Their supporters particularly pointed out how happy they look in the video.

“Happiness looks so good on you,” one user wrote.

“You look so gorgeous and refreshing. we love seeing how amazing you are doing,” another follower wrote.

Meanwhile, one fan stated how proud they are to see that Lovato is doing everything to heal themself. On the other hand, a follower recalled how it was unlikely for the singer to like baking before.

“Remember when you thought you could burn water, you’ve come so far,” the person wrote.

While the “Cool for the Summer” hitmaker has been enjoying their time in the kitchen, they’re also not leaving music behind. They recently released the live performance of their “Sorry Not Sorry (Rock Version).”
