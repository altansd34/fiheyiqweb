'AGT' Alum Kristy Sellars Flaunts Sparkling Barbie Pole Dance Choreo

TALENTRECAP.COM Talent America's Got Talent

Trae Patton/NBC

Reading Time: 3 minutes

America’s Got Talent runner up Kristy Sellars has decided to ride the Barbie craze. The dancer recently shared a glittering pole dance choreography of the classic “Barbie Girl” song. 

Kristy Sellars Shows Off Enticing Barbie Pole Dance

On July 16, Sellars posted a short video of her dancing to a slowed rendition of “Barbie Girl.” The clip was edited with colorful and sparkling diamond effects, imitating the vibe of Barbie‘s world.

“Barbie Girl. Couldn’t help myself with all this barbie fever!” she wrote.

Wearing a black top and leggings, Sellars paired her dancing outfit with a pair of dangerously high pink boots. The beginning of the clip showed Sellars effortlessly emulating Barbie’s movements. The AGT finalist smoothly executes her steps until the end, thus creating an amusing choreography worth watching once more.

“Awww I LOVE this! I love how you started with the kind of robotic realistic Barbie movements then slowly came to life!” one user wrote in the comments.

“Oh seriously, this is so cool! Love love love it!!!” another person wrote.

With her self-choreographed Barbie dance, Sellars took the time to promote her pole training app. Apparently, the Instagram video was only a snippet as the pole dancer has created a full routine which can be accessed in the said mobile app.

She Recently Reflected on Her AGT Journey

Last week, Sellars took the time to reminisce about her finale moment on America’s Got Talent season 17. The show’s alum posted a past photo of her performance and shared her thoughts on the same.

“Flashback to my America’s Got Talent finale performance,” she wrote. “I often can’t watch my performances back right away and enjoy them. I’m too busy analysing every micro movement that wasn’t exactly as I planned. But with time I start to appreciate and enjoy them more. This one really was such a beautiful moment.”

Finishing the competition in second place, Sellars came across as one of season 17’s fan-favorites. Such recent post has apparently made fans recount on the moment as well, recalling their awe with Sellars’ pole dance act.

“Girl, you got me crying like a child.. I didn’t just see your performance, I FELT it!! You touched my heart, reminded me of where I was and what I got out from in such a beautiful way… I felt every step in your performance,” one fan wrote. 

At present, Sellars is still actively pursuing her career in entertainment. The pole dancer has had her schedules jam-packed with a number of booked performances in various clubs and casinos.
