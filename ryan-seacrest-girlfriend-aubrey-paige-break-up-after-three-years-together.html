American Idol host Ryan Seacrest has reportedly broken up with his girlfriend, model Aubrey Paige Petcosky, after three years of dating. According to a source, the couple plans to remain friends post-split.

Ryan Seacrest, Girlfriend Aubrey Paige Break Up

People broke the news of Seacrest’s split from Petcosky on Wednesday, quoting a source who said the couple “mutually decided to part ways,” adding, “They plan to stay in each other’s lives as good friends and support one another’s endeavors.”

A representative for Seacrest apparently confirmed the breakup. He and Paige first went public with their romance in May 2021, when they were seen together celebrating Memorial Day in the Hamptons. In February of this year, they posted about their Valentine’s Day together.

Earlier this month, Seacrest promoted Petcosky’s new wine-themed card game Sippin on Somethin’ on Instagram. He wrote that he was “so proud” of Petcosky “for bringing this great idea to life.” The post included a selfie of the couple holding game cards.

Petcosky Celebrates ‘Rebirth’ on Instagram

Earlier this week, Petcosky took to Instagram to share a video of herself looking out the window and petting her dog, set to the song “End of Beginning” by Djo. The 26-year-old captioned the clip, “Rebirth.” She added a butterfly emoji.

Then on Wednesday, Petcosky posted a video in which she wears a bathing suit and sips wine in the pool. She captioned the post, “S/O to my haters, this one’s for you.” It’s unclear if either of these posts are referring to her breakup with Seacrest.

Seacrest is certainly keeping busy post-split, as he’s currently hosting American Idol Season 22, as well as his radio show. In September, Seacrest will be taking over hosting duties on Wheel of Fortune from the show’s current host Pat Sajak.
