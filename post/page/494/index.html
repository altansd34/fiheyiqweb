This data set provides real-time insights into how public trust is shifting in the country. The latest survey was conducted Dec. 5-8, 2023, among a representative sample of 2,201 U.S. adults, with an unweighted margin of error of +/-2 percentage points. U.S. Senator and Governor Approval Ratings

published on August 04, 2024
Since the beginning of 2017, Morning Consult has asked every registered U.S. voter we survey what they think of the job performance of the U.S. president, their U.S. senators and their governor. By consolidating responses from these daily interviews, we are able to provide the industry’s only comprehensive look at the popularity of America’s most important elected officials in all 50 states. Our definitive rankings of America’s most and least popular governors and senators provide the exceedingly rare apples-to-apples, cross-country comparison of politicians’ relative popularity among their own voter base, including by political affiliation, age, race and educational attainment.
