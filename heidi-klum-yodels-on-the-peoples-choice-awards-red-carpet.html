America’s Got Talent judge Heidi Klum attended the People’s Choice Awards on Tuesday night, and she took a moment to show off her yodeling skills during an interview on the red carpet. The supermodel also asked for suggestions for her 2023 Halloween costume.

Heidi Klum Yodels at the People’s Choice Awards

Klum chatted with Laverne Cox for E! News on Tuesday night’s red carpet. Cox asked Klum what her talent would be if she she were to compete on AGT. Klum answered, “I would yodel and make meatballs at the same time.”

She added that she loves to yodel, so Cox asked her to demonstrate. Klum shared a few seconds of the talent, then started laughing because people were looking at her. It’s not the first time Klum has shown off the skill. She even tried to teach fellow judge Howie Mandel how to yodel a few years ago.

Unfortunately for Klum and her fellow judges, America’s Got Talent didn’t take home the award for Competition Show of 2022. That honor went to fellow NBC talent show The Voice, which won the category for a seventh time.

Klum Is Already Thinking About Halloween 2023

Klum is known for her elaborate Halloween costumes, and this year was no exception, as she dressed up as a hyperrealistic worm. The model told E! that she’s “already thinking about next year.” But it sounds like she doesn’t have an idea in mind yet.

“Actually, I should put it out there,” Klum said to the camera. “Send me your ideas. I need new ideas.”

Klum starts preparing her Halloween costumes much earlier than most people probably would. This year, for example, she posted about preparation for the holiday all the way back in June. We can’t wait to find out what she has up her sleeve for 2023.
