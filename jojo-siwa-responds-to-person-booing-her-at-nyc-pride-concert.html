JoJo Siwa was recently booed by an audience member during a Pride event in New York City. Rather than ignore the heckler, Siwa chose to pause her performance and call them out — dropping an F-bomb in the process.

JoJo Siwa Responds to Person Booing Her at Pride Concert

According to People, Siwa took the stage at Trixie Mattel’s Pride Disco show in Central Park on Saturday. In a moment captured on video and posted on social media, Siwa can be seen shouting into the microphone, “Who the living f*ck just booed me?”

She goes on to demand the heckler identify themselves. Siwa then grabs a homemade sign from an audience member and says, “Respectfully, f*ck you.” She then raises her middle finger as the crowd cheers her on.

A few seconds later, someone in the audience throws a bouquet of flowers on stage, which Siwa picks up and calls “sweet.” She then sticks one of the roses in her shorts. After a moment, she tells the crowd, “F*ck the booers, somebody just chucked a bra at me.”

Siwa Announces Her Debut EP

On Monday, Siwa took to Instagram to announce that her debut EP, titled Guilty Pleasure, will be released July 12. The title track will have an accompanying music video. The other songs on the project include “Balance Baby,” “Yesterdays Tomorrows Today,” “Choose UR Fighter,” and “Karma.”

Siwa teased the music video for “Guilty Pleasure,” in which she wears the same outfit she wore for her recent performance in Central Park. It features Teddy bear heads on her chest and on top of her head. She can be seen lounging on a couch with another Teddy bear on the wall behind her.
