20 Pictures That Are All About the Angle

A picture can be difficult to understand for many different reasons: maybe you thought 2 people were at odds when they actually weren’t, the framing was bad, or the lighting was far from ideal. These are just examples of things that, in the end, show how important perspective is. This last case is something that happens quite often, due to taking the picture from the wrong perspective, and because of that, the photo turns out to be hilarious.

Many photographers who were stunned after freezing a moment shared their shots online, and at Bright Side, as usual, we put together a list of the best of them.

1. “Who let this panther in my house?”

2. “This photo I took of a woman with a very long arm”

3. “This is a giant hole, but it kind of just looks like a little hole in the ground with wooden floor panels.”

4. “I have been looking at their hands for 5 minutes and still have no clue who’s hand it is on the left.”

5. “Which way does it go?”

6. “My dogs have merged together.”

7. “An island in the sky or a glassy lake?”

8. “A dog reading a pamphlet on the bus”

9. A “snake” with some mildew

10. “Mom, I think I broke the door.”

11. Your pet doesn’t understand what it’s seeing either.

12. “My friend easily carrying his end of this fallen tree”

13. A mountain or a river?

14. A levitating box

15. “A giant shipment of Yorkshire Tea”

16. “Believe it or not, this is the back, where you put your face.”

17. “The Goblin Under the Stairs”

18. “My sister took this pic today.”

19. “Just a cow, its name is Bertha.”

20. “Dirt bike”

Which images, beyond perspective, have you had a hard time understanding?
