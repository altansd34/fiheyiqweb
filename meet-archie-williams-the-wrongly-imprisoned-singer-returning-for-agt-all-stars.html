Meet Archie Williams, The Wrongly Imprisoned Singer Returning for 'AGT All-Stars'

TALENTRECAP.COM Talent America's Got Talent: All-Stars

Photo by: Trae Patton/NBC

Reading Time: 2 minutes

In 2020, Archie Williams’ vocals were introduced to America. Not only did he shock the national television through his voice, he shared his devastating years of experience in jail. In 2023, he aims to bring back his inspiring story in AGT All-Stars.

Archie Williams Spent Decades in Jail for a Crime He Didn’t Commit

On April 21, 1983, Archie Williams was wrongfully accused of raping and stabbing a woman in Louisiana. At the time, no strong evidence would ever tell he’s guilty, but his liberty was nevertheless taken away. Devastatingly, Williams had to suffer the punishment of life imprisonment without possibility of parole.

To cut the story short, he spent approximately 37 years incarcerated, hopeless that the court would believe his innocence. Throughout his years in jail, music has been his comfort. He joined the prison’s church choir, where he sang his heart out. He also founded his own band with other inmates, finding solace whenever people would come to hear his voice.

Williams was finally out of jail in 2019. As a compensation for his wrongful incarceration, he became entitled to a maximum of $250,000. Unfortunately, money wasn’t enough for Williams to bring his whole life back. Thus, he sought for another chance to live through America’s Got Talent season 15.

He Made Heidi Klum Tear Up During His AGT Audition

Williams’ audition consisted of Elton John’s “Don’t Let The Sun Go Down On Me,” which received a standing ovation from the judges and the audience. Judge Heidi Klum, who admitted that she wasn’t a crybaby, teared up by the end of his performance.

“I felt you, and I’ve been thinking about you and your life. That was very powerful,” she said.

Williams was automatically sent straight to the Quarterfinals, where he belted Stevie Wonder’s “Love’s In Need of Love Today.” After garnering enough votes for this round, he advanced to the competition’s Semifinals. He captured America’s heart until the AGT’s Finale, ending up in the Top 10.

Archie Williams Returns for AGT All-Stars

While Williams wasn’t hailed as the season 15 winner, but he surely touched and inspired everyone with his journey. Today, he continues to seek justice for innocent people, like him, who are still in jail. As he does, he will attempt to gain another success on television through AGT All-Stars.

Williams is among the 60 talented acts who will vie for the winning spot in this upcoming AGT spin-off series. To witness how far he’ll go in this competition, tune into NBC on January 30.
