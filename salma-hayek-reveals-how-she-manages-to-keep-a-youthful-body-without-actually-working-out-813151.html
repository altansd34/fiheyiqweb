Salma Hayek Reveals How She Manages to Keep a Youthful Body Without Actually Working Out

Hayek’s recent Instagram photos are so hot they could make the ocean boil. Despite bikini season officially starting next month, celebrities are already sharing their beach-ready looks on social media, and Salma is no exception. Fans are raving about her recent Instagram post, and we also can’t help but admire her youthful physique.

Salma Hayek shares lots of bikini photos on Instagram.

Salma Hayek Pinault isn’t shy about flaunting her fabulous figure. She’s been posting a ton of swimwear snaps on social media and has no plans to quit anytime soon. Every pic of hers shows Hayek’s toned abs and sculpted arms, which makes everyone wonder what she does to stay fit.

Everyone is amazed by her shape.

The star surprises everyone with her slim body, and many comment on her ability to age backward. Hayek wrote on Instagram, “Every time I need to feel renewed, I jump into the ocean.” Other celebrities like Cindy Crawford, Paris Hilton, and Kerry Washington also take notice of her stunning shape and leave comments.

Her comment section blows up.

Fans are amazed at Salma’s great shape. One user wrote, “Looking this young in your 50s should be illegal, like what.” Another asked, “So if I jump into the ocean, then I’ll come out looking like you?” Someone even exclaimed, “My only goal in life is to look good as her!”

Salma almost never works out.

Salma admits that she rarely works out since she works more than 16 hours a day — sometimes, long days can even last for 20 hours. Her trainer taught her to activate her muscles throughout the day. The actress shares, “She taught me how to hold my body in a way where the muscles are activated all day long.” The star explains the technique involves toning muscles without clenching them.

She makes sure to stay active during the day.

The actress also practices restorative yoga from time to time or dances to make her occasional workouts more fun. Her dogs are also her “trainers,” she loves going on walks with them. What’s more, she adores swimming, swears by massage, and loves juices.

The actress loves food.

The star says, “Everybody has a weakness and mine is food. If you love food, and they put you in France, you’re in a good place, and you’re in a bad place at the same time.” Hayek is a huge fan of Latin cuisine and her favorite restaurant is the award-winning Pujol in Mexico City. She can’t visit as often as she’d like, as she lives far away, so she often whips up her own versions of Mexican dishes for dinner.
