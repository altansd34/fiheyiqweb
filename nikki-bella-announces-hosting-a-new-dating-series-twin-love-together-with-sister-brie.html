Nikki Bella Announces Hosting a New Dating Series 'Twin Love' Together With Sister Brie

Nikki Bella recently announced that she and her twin sister, Brie, will be hosting a new dating competition series on Amazon called Twin Love. The news has been met with excitement from fans of the Bella sisters and dating shows alike.

Nikki Bella is Set to Host Twin Love With Twin Sister Brie

Amazon has greenlit a new dating competition series titled Twin Love which will be hosted by former WWE stars Brie and Nikki Bella, according to a recent announcement. The show will be available to stream on Prime Video in the US and UK. It will also be premiered on the free streaming platform Freevee.

In her Instagram post announcing the new show, Bella expressed her excitement and gratitude for the opportunity to host Twin Love with her sister. She also hinted that the show would have some surprises in store for viewers.

The show will focus on the love lives of identical twins. With ten sets of twins being separated and placed in two houses with identical casts. The twins will then begin a search for love in a unique and captivating social experiment. The series will explore whether their inherent similarities extend to their romantic desires, as they are separated from their twin siblings for the first time.

Brie and Nikki Bella are no strangers to reality TV. Having appeared in several popular series including E!’s Total Divas and Total Bellas. Amazon’s Twin Love dating show premieres this summer as their latest bid for success in the genre. Following previous shows like The One That Got Away and Cosmic Love.

Nikki Bella Makes a Bold Claim About Total Divas

The former WWE superstar is regarded as one of the most adored female wrestlers to have graced the company. She is recognized as one of the key figures of the Women’s Evolution in WWE. Having played an important role in pioneering the women’s division.

Apart from her professional achievements, Bella’s personal life has also been the talk of the town. Her appearance on Total Divas, a reality TV show about WWE‘s female wrestlers, and its spin-off, Total Bellas, drew mainstream attention to the company.

During a recent interview, Bella reflected on the show’s success and how it proved that female WWE superstars could compete with the best in the business. She expressed disappointment that the industry did not offer more support. Instead using the show to criticize her and her castmates for their outside endeavors.

Bella emphasized that Total Divas should have been praised more, given the show’s groundbreaking contributions to women’s wrestling. It had the potential to shake things up and create a positive change, but the industry failed to rally behind the stars. Despite this, Bella’s legacy in WWE continues to be celebrated. Her fans also remain devoted to her even after her departure from the company.
