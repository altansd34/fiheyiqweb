11Revolutionary Models Who Prove Each ofUsDeserves toBeAdmired

The fashion industry is changing: doll-like models that were popular in the past are giving way to people whose beauty is unusual and original.

Bright Side supports this tendency toward diversity. Here are the most unusual and famous models of today.

Bee ​​​​​​​Melvnin

Bee Melvnin is from Gambia and her amazing appearance attracts a lot of attention. Her very dark skin together with her light hair looks absolutely stunning. She has over 400,000 subscribers on her Instagram page.

Molly Bair

Molly Bair is known as the “Alien Girl.” She has an unusual face and even once said, “I would never think that a girl who spent most of her childhood with a unibrow, glasses, and a Yoda shirt would be in Vogue Italia.”

Casil McArthur

“It takes a lot of courage to be the person you want to be,” transgender model Casil McArthur says. Marc Jacobs and Calvin Klein absolutely love her. Casil was born a female, but at the age of 16, he started working in the fashion industry as a man.

Adwoa Aboah

Adwoa Aboah is attractive mostly thanks to her sincerity. She has always been very open when she talks about her drug addiction and she criticizes the stereotypes around the modeling business. Every time she is on the runway she proves that it is much better to be who you are, than to be perfect.

Natalie Westling

Natalie Westling made Prada and Christian Dior fall in love with her. She’s also done photo shoots for Vogue, AnOther Magazine, i-D, and Dazed.

Diana Veras

Diana Veras is proud of her body and hair, she doesn’t hide her cellulite, and she doesn’t follow the trends. She’s appeared on the covers of Harper’s Bazaar and Teen Vogue. And she has one of the most honest Instagram pages, where she showcases her life without Photoshop.

Tierra Ouellette

Tierra Ouellette has become one of the key starters of the Anti-Agency, which specializes in searching for girls with unorthodox beauty. Her career has barely started, but we are sure that Ouellette has everything it takes to become a famous model.

Scarlett Costello

Scarlett Costello’s unibrow is her key feature. She was laughed at when she was in school, but today her classmates must be really jealous of her career when they see her in Teen Vogue and Cosmopolitan.

Isabel Hendrix

Plus-size model Isabel Hendrix loves changing her hair color and is sure that fashion is not supposed to have any rules.

Stella Lucia

This young model from Austria says that fashion is her way of expressing herself and who she is as a person. Photo shoots for Tom Ford and Alexander Wang, fashion shows for Gucci, Chanel, and Fendi prove that Stella Lucia is great at what she does.

Jazzelle Zanaughtti

Rebellious Jazzelle Zanaughtti loves experimenting with her appearance and is not afraid of showing her character. This courage has made her a star and one of the brightest models of today.

Which of these models do you like the best?
