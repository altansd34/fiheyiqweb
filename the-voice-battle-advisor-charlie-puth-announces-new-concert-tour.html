Former The Voice Battle Advisor Charlie Puth recently announced an upcoming new concert tour called “Charlie” the Live Experience. Tickets for the tour (which kicks off in May) go on sale to the general public this Friday.

Charlie Puth Announces New Concert Tour

On Tuesday, Puth shared a poster promoting the new tour, featuring an upside-down black and white photo of him. In the caption of the Instagram post, the singer shared that fans can text him at 323.524.9978 for pre-sale access. Otherwise, tickets are on sale this Friday, February 17.

The first stop of Puth’s tour is Guadalajara, Mexico, as part of the Corona Capital music festival in May. After that, there are dates lined up throughout the United States and Canada, running into July. You can visit Puth’s website for a full list of dates and links to buy tickets.

This is Puth’s second tour since releasing his third studio album, titled Charlie, last year. He previously set out on the One Night Only tour starting in October. Charlie featured singles such as “That’s Hilarious” and “Light Switch.”

Puth Was a Battle Advisor Last Season on The Voice

Last year, Puth appeared on Season 22 of The Voice as a Battle Advisor on coach Camila Cabello’s team. The singer offered his experience and expertise to help Cabello’s team members prepare for their Battle round performances.

Puth had previously appeared on The Voice as a Battle Advisor to Alicia Keys in Season 11 and Adam Levine in Season 16. He was also a musical guest on the NBC songwriting show Songland in 2019, so he’s no stranger to talent shows.

The signer told Extra that he would “one hundred percent” consider becoming a coach on The Voice, saying, “I love listening to people sing and I love offering any bit of musical advice that I could offer, so I feel really honored and I feel very fortunate to be able to do this with Camila.”
