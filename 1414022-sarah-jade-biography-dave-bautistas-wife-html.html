Career

Sarah got into pole dancing when she was 18 years old after watching YouTube videos of popular dancers Karol Hems and Alethea Austin in action.

Jade performed at the Ink-N-Iron Showcase of the Stars and started doing routines with famous aerial performers like Jamilla Deville. She also took years of dance training under the tutelage of Zoraya Jude.

Jade has worked on numerous projects, including the Florida Pole & Aerial Arts Showcase, Miss Pole Dance America, and Pole Show LA.

She placed second at the USPDF Amateur Nationals in 2012. Jade also competed at the Florida Pole Fitness Championship (Pro Division), where she earned the 'Most Athletic' award in 2012 and 2013. Sarah was named Miss Pole Dance America in 2016.
