13 Celebrities Truly Honest Opinion About Their Own Plastic Surgeries

Celebrities have to spend a lot of time in front of cameras and often have a team that helps them look their best at different events. And in everyday life, they stick to a healthy diet, regular exercise, proper sleep, and get help from beauty experts and, in some cases, plastic surgeons.

Lisa Kudrow

Sipa USA / Sipa USA / East News

The Friends star had a nose job when she was 16 years old. She did it the summer before going to a new high school where no one knew how she looked before.

Jane Fonda

AF Archive / Mary Evans Picture Library / East News, CHRIS DELMAS / AFP / East News

Hollywood legend Jane Fonda believes that plastic surgery helped her shed 10 years. The actress admits that people looked older in the past, but with all the opportunities we have today, she decided to finally say no to plastic surgery when she turned 82.

Katy Perry

Elizabeth Goodenough / Everett Collection / East News

The celebrity says that her body curves are natural, but she did admit to getting filler injections under her eyes.

Olivia Colman

Joel Ryan / Invision / AP / East News, East News

Academy Award-winner Olivia Colman admits that she’s used Botox. The actress had Botox injections in 2015 for the first time when there was a gap between roles. Colman didn’t say anything about it to her husband, but he saw a difference.

Cardi B

Grosby Group / East News

Bodacious rapper turned to plastic surgeons many times. In 2018, she had a breast lift and liposuction after giving birth. Before that, Cardi B had breast augmentation surgery. She had dreamt of bigger breasts since the age of 16.

Yolanda Hadid

AFP / East News

Yolanda Hadid left plastic surgery and cosmetic injections behind. When she turned 55, she admitted that she had had breast augmentation surgery and used Botox and fillers. But she stopped getting them after being diagnosed with Lyme. Now Hadid feels happy, letting her body free from what society conditioned her to believe a beautiful woman should look like.

Amy Schumer

The comedian spoke openly about the liposuction she had. She confessed that she went under the knife because of a combination of factors, like her difficult pregnancy, giving birth, her struggle with endometriosis, and ultimately having her uterus and appendix removed.

Kelly Rowland

PHOTOlink / Everett Collection / East News, James Atoa / Everett Collection / East News

Singer Kelly Rowland dreamed of having her breasts augmented at 18 years old when she was in Destiny’s Child. Beyoncé’s mother, Tina Knowles, advised her to think thoroughly before taking this step. The singer followed her advice.

Rowland took her time and wore special bras to understand what it was like to have bigger breasts. At the age of 28, Rowland got the plastic surgery she wanted.

Kris Jenner

Kris Jenner made a fortune on showing the life of her family to the world. In one of the episodes of Keeping up with the Kardashians, she documented a facelift she got before her daughter Kim Kardashian’s second wedding.

Jillian Michaels

To make her nose more refined, she turned to plastic surgery.

It’s worth mentioning that these changes didn’t add confidence to the celebrity. To become self-confident, Michaels needed therapy, which she’s been using since the age of 5.

Cindy Crawford

Dee Cercone / Everett Collection / East News

The supermodel who turned 56 this year retains her beauty not only thanks to a healthy diet and exercising, but she also gets Botox, vitamin, and collagen injections. The celebrity has been getting cosmetic injections since the age of 29.

Gwyneth Paltrow

Plastic surgery is a personal choice, and individuals should not feel pressured to undergo any procedures that they are uncomfortable with. Anyway, the beautiful Gwyneth Paltrow has openly discussed her personal experiences with plastic surgery. Paltrow has expressed her interest in using organic products and laser treatments to maintain a balanced lifestyle, but she has also stated that she would be hesitant to undergo surgery. “I would be scared to go under the knife, but, you know, talk to me when I’m 50”.

Additionally, Paltrow has shared her experience with Botox, revealing that she would not use it again due to the negative effects it had on her appearance. As she mentions, “I’ll try anything. Except I won’t do Botox again.”

Courteney Cox

Courteney Cox revealed that she had a realization about the injections and other facial treatments she has undergone in the past. She admitted that she never noticed how strange they made her look until recently. She stated: “I didn’t realize that I’m actually looking really strange with injections and doing stuff to my face that I would never do now.”
