Popular Nigerian singer Nomoreloss is dead

There are reports that popular Nigerian singer, Olumuyiwa Osinuga, popularly called Nomoreloss,  is dead.

Unconfirmed sources say that the ‘Iyawo Asiko’ crooner who is in his early 40s died this morning, March 21, at an undisclosed hospital in Opebi, Ikeja Lagos.
