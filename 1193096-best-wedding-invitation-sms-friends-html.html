How do you write a wedding invitation message?

If you do not have a way with words, you can always choose and alter some already existing messages to suit your preferences. You can remove text and insert names, dates, and locations as you wish. Do not forget to mention the time and venue of the wedding ceremony.

What to say on a wedding invitation?

Here is a quick look at some of the information that conventionally appears in wedding invitations.

  • Any relevant information about the venue
  • Attire details
  • Ceremony and reception location
  • RSVP card
  • The couple's names
  • The date and time of the ceremony

What does RSVP stand for?

The abbreviation can be found at the bottom of most wedding invitation cards. It means Répondez s'il Vous plait, which is French for 'respond, if you please,' or just 'please respond.'

How to invite my friends for my wedding?

Here is something you can message your guests to enquire about their attendance. "Hello, I hope you had a chance to see our wedding invitation and RSVP. The deadline is coming up soon; if you have not done so, please let me know."
