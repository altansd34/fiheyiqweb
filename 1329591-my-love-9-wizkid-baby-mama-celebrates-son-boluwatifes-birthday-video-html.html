Nigerian singer, Wizkid, seems to have buried the hatchet he has with his baby mama, Oluwanishola Ogudu, as he acknowledged his son Boluwatife's birthday on social media.

Boluwatife clocked nine on May 13, 2020, and Wizkid, took to his Twitter page to celebrate the occasion. The music star referred to Boluwatife as his first love.

According to the Star Boy, his first love has clocked nine. The singer also referred to him with an endearing tone by tweeting: ‘Boluwatife mi’, meaning ‘my Boluwatife’.
