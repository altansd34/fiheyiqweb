Forever Yours, Michael J. Fox Honors Wife Tracy Pollan on Their 35th Anniversary

Michael J. Fox and his wife, Tracy Pollan, have an extraordinary bond built on love and support. Tracy has consistently been there for Michael, standing by his side through every twist and turn. On their 35th anniversary, Michael J. Fox commemorates his wife in the most touching way imaginable.

Michael J. Fox and Tracy Pollan are joyfully commemorating a remarkable achievement.

The beloved star of Back to the Future, aged 62, and his talented actress wife, aged 63, expressed their love and appreciation for each other on Instagram as they celebrated their 35th anniversary.

Michael J. Fox took the opportunity to write a heartfelt anniversary tribute to his wife, accompanied by a collection of nostalgic photos capturing the couple embracing each other tightly.

“Forever yours, Mike...”

“35 years of laughter, living, listening, and loving you. Thank you for it all,” J. Fox described the lovely photos. Pollan warmly replied to her husband’s affectionate message, expressing her love by writing, “Love you so much!!!”

Actress Jennifer Grey joined in to extend her well-wishes on their posts, leaving comments like “love you guys” and “two of my favorite people.” Even Clark Gregg, star of Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D. and Grey’s ex-husband, sent his love with a sweet message saying, “You guys are ❤️ goals!”

Pollan also decided to share the joy with her beloved followers on Instagram.

“35 years!!!” exclaimed Pollan in the caption of her post, accompanied by a charming throwback photo of the two Bright Lights, Big City stars enjoying a vacation together. She expressed her heartfelt anniversary wishes, saying, “Happy anniversary, my love. Here’s to many more glorious adventures together. I could not love you more.”

“Likewise, so beautiful just like you,” Fox commented on the vacation photo. “Happy Anniversary!”

Michael J. Fox and his wife, Tracy Pollan, share a strong bond of love and support. Tracy has been a constant pillar of strength for Michael, offering her unconditional support through thick and thin. Her presence and understanding have inspired him, helping him overcome challenges with grace and determination.
